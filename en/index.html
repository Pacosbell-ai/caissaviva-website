Our Mission

Chess as a tool for holistic development

The CaissaViva Foundation promotes educational chess as a transformative tool for the intellectual, social, and emotional development of children and adolescents in the Dominican Republic.

We believe every move on the board teaches a life lesson: think before you act, accept consequences, and never give up. We work with educational institutions to integrate chess into the education of young people aged 5 to 18, building reflective, disciplined, and collaborative citizens.

Our Programs

Chess for every stage of growth

Structured and progressive programs, tailored to each developmental level, taught by instructors certified by FIDE and the Dominican Chess Federation.

Ages 3 – 5

Playful Introduction

Introduction to chess through games, stories, and motor activities that awaken strategic curiosity from the earliest years.

  • 1 session per week
  • Curricular format
  • Play-based learning
Ages 6 – 10

Chess Fundamentals

Learning rules, basic tactics, and first games. Concentration and logical thinking are progressively developed.

  • 2 sessions per week
  • Extracurricular activity
  • Monthly internal tournaments
Ages 11 – 14

Competitive Development

Intermediate training with emphasis on openings, middle game, and endgames. Preparation for school and national tournaments.

  • 2 sessions per week, 1.5h each
  • Groups of up to 15 students
  • Participation in FDA/AEFE tournaments
Ages 15 – 18

High Performance

Advanced training with projection to national and international tournaments. In-depth game analysis and competitive preparation.

  • 2 sessions per week, 1.5h each
  • Personalized technical coaching
  • Representation in international tournaments

Our Vision

Expanding chess across the Dominican Republic

Strategic initiatives to bring chess to every corner of the country and transform the lives of thousands of Dominican youth.

01

Chess in Public Schools

Partnerships with the Ministry of Education to integrate chess as an extracurricular activity in extended-day schools nationwide.

02

National School Circuit

Creating a regional school tournament circuit culminating in an annual national youth championship, fostering identity and healthy competition.

03

Digital Chess Platform

Developing an online platform with interactive lessons, virtual tournaments, and progress tracking for students in remote areas.

04

Chess Talent Scholarships

Scholarship program for talented youth from vulnerable communities, covering training, materials, and participation in international tournaments.

05

Summer Camps

Intensive chess camps during vacation periods in different provinces, combining sport, culture, and fellowship among youth from across the country.

06

Instructor Training

Certifying new Dominican instructors through FIDE-standard training programs, multiplying reach in every community.
